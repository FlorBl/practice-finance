box-sizing:border-box;  If 2 divs have the same height/width but one looks biggers because of its padding etc. This fixes the problem.

width: auto;    Default value. The browser calculates the width.

overflow-x: hidden;    Hide the horizontal scrollbar for overflow content

overflow-y: hidden;    Hide the vertical scrollbar for overflow content

height: auto;   the element will automatically adjust its height to allow its content to be displayed correctly.

Flex
display: flex;     Aligns divs from left to right.
flex-wrap: wrap;   Aligns divs from left to right, wraps them when the page gets smaller, they keep the same size.
flex-wrap: wrap;    A container with "flex-wrap: nowrap;" will never wrap its items.
